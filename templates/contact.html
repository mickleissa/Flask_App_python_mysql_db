FAQ

How To Cancel Booking?

To cancel your booking, simply log in to your account, navigate to your bookings, and select the option to cancel. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team for help

When Will I Get The Possession?

The possession date will be communicated to you upon completion of all necessary procedures and documentation. For any specific inquiries regarding possession, please reach out to our customer service team for assistance

Where Can I Pay The Rent?

You can conveniently pay your rent through our online portal or at our leasing office. For further details or assistance with rent payment options, feel free to contact our leasing team

How To Contact With The Buyers?

To connect with potential buyers, you can utilize our platform's messaging feature or reach out to them directly via the contact information provided. If you need assistance or have any questions about contacting buyers, don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team.

Why My Listing Not Showing Up?

If your listing is not showing up, it may be due to various factors such as incomplete information or a pending review process. Please ensure all necessary details are provided and wait for our team to review and approve your listing. If you continue to experience issues, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.

How To Promote My Listing?

To promote your listing, you can utilize our platform's promotional tools such as featured listings or sponsored ads. Additionally, sharing your listing on social media platforms and reaching out to your network can also help increase visibility. For personalized strategies and assistance in promoting your listing, feel free to contact our marketing team
